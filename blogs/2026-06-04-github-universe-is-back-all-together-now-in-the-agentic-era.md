GitHub Universe returns to Fort Mason Center in San Francisco on October 28-29, 2026, bringing together developers and technical leaders for two days focused on AI agents and collaborative software development. The event features new formats including Ship & Tell lightning talks, Speaker After Parties, and expanded open source programming. Early-bird registration is available with significant savings before July 9.
